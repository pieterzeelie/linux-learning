Linux is an **open-source, community-driven operating system** inspired by UNIX, while UNIX is a **proprietary, enterprise-focused system** developed in the 1970s.  
- **Linux** dominates modern computing (cloud, servers, IoT, supercomputers).  
- **UNIX** remains in legacy enterprise systems (banking, telecom, government).
